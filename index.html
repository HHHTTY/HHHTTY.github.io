Research Focus 🔬

Multimodal scientific intelligence across spectra, molecules, and 3D perception.

Molecular Structure Elucidation

Learning stable, interpretable links between molecular structures and multi-spectral evidence such as NMR, MS, and IR signals.

Multimodal Foundation Models

Designing mixture-of-experts and hierarchical representation learning frameworks that fuse heterogeneous scientific modalities.

3D Point Cloud Understanding

Self-supervised and multi-view learning for robust 3D scene and object understanding from point clouds and aligned modalities.
