About Me 👋

I am a Ph.D. student in the Data Science and Analytics Thrust at HKUST(GZ), advised by Prof. Jun Xia. My research develops multimodal learning systems for scientific discovery, with a particular focus on molecular structure elucidation from spectroscopy, stable mixture-of-experts learning, and 3D point cloud understanding.

Before joining HKUST(GZ), I received my M.Eng. degree from Southeast University, where I worked on multimodal representation learning at PALM Lab. I also spent time at Huawei 2012 Laboratories, working on AI chip scheduling optimization, operator acceleration, and intelligent driving perception systems.

Research Interests 🔬

My research focuses on building learning systems that connect heterogeneous scientific evidence with reliable representations, interpretable structure, and scalable deployment. Current interests include:

  • Molecular structure elucidation: learning links between molecular structures and multi-spectral evidence such as NMR, MS, and IR signals.
  • Multimodal foundation models: designing mixture-of-experts and hierarchical representation learning frameworks for scientific modalities.
  • 3D point cloud understanding: self-supervised and multi-view learning for robust 3D scene and object understanding.
